# Objectives

By the end of the project, everyone should understand:

- **What an ERP is** and why the P300 occurs.
- **Why averaging improves ERP detection.**
- **Core MNE-Python data structures** — what `Raw` objects, events, epochs, and evoked responses are.
- **Filtering & baseline correction** — why they matter and how they shape the signal.
- **Visualization** — how to plot and interpret ERP waveforms and topographies.
